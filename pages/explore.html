SDG Goal 11 Philippine-based Indicators

Documentary Feature: Sa Layag ng Bangkang Paurong

Urbanization and Its Impact on Filipino Communities

"Sa Layag ng Bangkang Paurong" is a 2019 documentary film by the Alaminos City National High School’s Layag Productions that explores how urbanization affects local communities in the Philippines. The film tells the story of Milboy, a young boatman from Alaminos City, which is home to the famous Hundred Islands. As the city grows and tourism increases, Milboy’s experience highlights the struggle between maintaining traditional ways of life and adapting to the pressures of a rapidly changing environment.

Urbanization brings new opportunities, but it also presents significant challenges. For young people like Milboy, the need to work and support their families often comes at the expense of education and future prospects. The documentary sheds light on these issues, showing how economic growth can lead to early employment, disrupted schooling, and the gradual loss of cultural heritage. This film adds a personal perspective to the broader discussion about the current state of cities in the Philippines. As urban areas expand, they often do so at the cost of social stability and cultural preservation. Milboy’s story is a reminder of the human impact behind these trends.
