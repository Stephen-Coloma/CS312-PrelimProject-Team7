What is SDG 11?

SDG 11 focuses on making cities inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable. It addresses the challenges of urbanization, including housing shortages, inadequate infrastructure, and environmental degradation.

Why is SDG 11 Important?

With over half of the world's population living in urban areas, sustainable development is crucial to ensuring a high quality of life for all residents.

SDG 11 in the Philippines

In the Philippines, rapid urbanization has led to a range of challenges, including traffic congestion, pollution, and housing shortages. SDG 11 aims to address these issues by promoting sustainable urban planning and development.
